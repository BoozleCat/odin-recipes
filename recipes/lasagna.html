Easy Lasagna

This lasagna is made in a skillet and is easier than baked.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (1 box)
  • Pasta sauce (1 jar)
  • Shredded cheese (8 oz)
  • Ground beef (1 lb)

Steps

  1. Place precooked noodles in skillet.
  2. Add browned ground beef.
  3. Cover with jar of sauce.
  4. Add shredded cheese.
  5. Cook on medium heat for 20 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
