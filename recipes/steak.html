Grilled Steak

This steak is lightly seasoned before being charred on a grill.

Ingredients

  • Sirloin filet (8 oz)
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Cracked sea salt

Steps

  1. Place steak on counter for 30 minutes to bring to room temp.
  2. Crack salt and then pepper on top of steak covering the surface.
  3. Flip steak over and add salt and pepper to other side.
  4. Preheat grill.
  5. Add steak to hot grill.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes and rotate steak 90 degrees.
  7. Cook for 2 minutes more and flip steak over.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes and rotate 90 degrees.
  9. Remove steak to plate and cover with foil.
  10. Let steak rest 5 minutes before cutting.
