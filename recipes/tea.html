Sweet Tea

This tea is lightly sweetened and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • Tea bags (3)
  • Baking soda (1/4 tsp)
  • Sugar (3/4 cup)
  • Water (3 qts)

Steps

  1. Pour water into iced tea maker.
  2. Add tea bags to filter.
  3. Add baking soda and sugar to pitcher.
  4. Turn on tea maker and wait for cycle to complete.
  5. Thoroughly stir tea.
  6. Serve over ice.
